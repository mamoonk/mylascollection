The Atelier

Couture, the slow way

A single Myla bridal can pass through more than a dozen pairs of hands — from the karchob frame where artisans lay each thread, to the final drape that takes weeks to perfect.

We work in small batches and made-to-measure orders, so the woman wearing the piece is never an afterthought. She is the design.

❦ The House of Myla

A love letter to
Pakistani craft

Founded by Myla as a single embroidery frame in old Lahore, our house has grown into a destination for women who want their heritage worn beautifully — never costume, always couture. From the everyday to the once-in-a-lifetime, every Myla piece is made to be remembered, and to be passed on.
